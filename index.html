About Me

Hi, I’m Appu Anand—a data analyst with a builder’s mindset and a storyteller’s instinct. I turn messy data into business wins through dashboards that drive action, anomaly detection that prevents losses, and insights that speak louder than noise.

With 2 years of hands-on experience, I’ve led Smart City ML annotation ops, built Power BI dashboards for QSR and retail clients, and developed fraud detection tools that hit 92% precision. My toolkit includes Python, SQL, Power BI, and Excel—but my real strength lies in connecting the dots between data and decisions.

I thrive at the intersection of curiosity and impact. Whether it’s segmenting customers with K-means, optimizing email campaigns through A/B testing, or diagnosing sales drops with DAX-powered dashboards—I’m always chasing clarity, speed, and results.

Outside the data world, I sketch game ideas, explore sci-fi universes, and experiment with new tech just for the thrill of it. I’m currently open to data-driven roles in product, marketing, operations, or analytics—anywhere I can keep building, learning, and solving real-world problems.

Let’s turn data into momentum.
